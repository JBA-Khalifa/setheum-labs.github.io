SerpUps: Cashback on Transactions

Cashback and Discounts are given on all SettPay transaction including non-Setheum tokens, cross-chain and multi-currency transactions. We call it SerpUp while others call it inflation, Serpups are given back to the community as cashback on SettPay and Setheum allocates 20% of every serpup to buy back and burn Dinar as a tribute to the Dinar and its validators.

SettinDEX Liquidity Incentives

Setheum's built-in Decentralized Exchange (Sett-in-DEX) has an allocated 10% of initial Setheum Dinar tokens (12,900,000 DNAR) as liquidity incentives to be distributed over a period of time to liquidity providers in certain pools.

Initial SettCurrency Offering (ISCO) Incentives

As in the case of the first ever ISCO, SettPay and the SettCurrencies/stablecoins will be launched after Setheum Network Substrate Chain Genesis, when the initial 129,000,000 DNAR is already in the market as an ERC20 token for some months. The ISCO will launch after DNAR-jDNAR liquid staking phase has launched. The ISCO will be a 30 days event in which jDNAR holders (DNAR stakers) are required to deposit their jDNAR tokens within the first 20 days as "pre-ISCO", DNAR can still be locked until the main DAY 1 ISCO (funds releasing) begins. Then the main ISCO will start in which settcurrencies are released to depositors within another 10 days through a FIFO queue where the first to deposit will be the first to be paid out in settcurrencies 10% of deposit value (value at main ISCO launch time) per day for the remaining 10 days. Then the locked LiquidDinar (jDNAR) will be released in full on the last day. The ISCO will issue SettCurrencies valued at 100% of all locked jDNAR at main ISCO launch time, 70% to the depositors and 30% to the SettPay system for initial SettPay and SettCard discounts and cashback.
